Console Operations Manual

  • Power Switch: Click the slider at the top left to power the console ON/OFF.
  • D-Pad Left / Select: Toggle Pikachu's dance and tail wagging.
  • D-Pad Right / Start: Toggle Day/Night cycle (watch the screen shift and stars appear!).
  • Button A: Trigger Thunderbolt attack (high-voltage screen shake and electricity!).
  • Button B: Send Pikachu to sleep (breathing slows down, eyes close, Zzz floats up).
